Design Principles

Hj Saguire

Visual Hierarchy

The first thing that will get your attention is the screen. Visual hierarchy refers to the order in which the human eye perceives what it sees. It describes which elements capture your user’s attention and draw their eyes most.

Repetition

On that picture, you will notice the design repeated many times. If you are using repeating elements as an area of relief in a design, variation is not necessary. If the repeating elements are to add to the interest of a design or painting, variation is important.

White Space

On that picture, there is a space that leads the person to make a conscious effort to add a design for emphasis and structure They also left out the white space intentionally to give more focus to the content and to delimit one element from the other.
